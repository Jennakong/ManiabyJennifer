Tribe & Trends

Born from a love of fabric, form and the women who wear them, MANIA by Jennifer tells stories in stitches. Tribe & Trends is a study in contrast – bold West African prints and textures, cut into clean, city-ready lines. Every piece is made in small runs, styled to be worn, re-worn and remembered.

From studio fittings to late–night sewing sessions, each garment has passed through Jennifer’s hands – measured, pressed and finished with care. No loud logos. Just clothes that hold their own.

Designed in Manchester · Rooted in West Africa · Made to last
